Change the Resolution (Windows®)

You can choose the print resolution. As print resolution and speed are related, the higher the resolution, the longer it will take to print the document.
  1. Select the print command in your application.
  2. Select Brother MFC-XXXX series (where XXXX is the name of your model), and then click the printing properties or preferences button.
    The printer driver window appears.
  3. Click the Resolution drop down list and click the print resolution you want to use.
    • 300 dpi
    • 600 dpi
    • HQ 1200
  4. Click OK.
  5. Complete your print operation.
