Change or Delete One Touch Dial Numbers

Related Models:MFC‑L2700D / MFC‑L2701D / MFC‑L2700DW / MFC‑L2701DW
You can change or delete One Touch Dial Numbers.
  1. Press Menu.
  2. Press or to display the Fax option, and then press OK.
  3. Press or to display the Address Book option, and then press OK.
  4. Press or to display the One Touch Dial option, and then press OK.
  5. Press the One Touch button you want to change or delete.
  6. Do one of the following:
    • To change the stored name and number:
      1. Press 1.
      2. Edit the number and name.

        To edit the stored name or number, press or to position the cursor under the character you want to change, and then press Clear.

        Enter the correct character, and then press OK.

    • To delete the stored name and number:
      1. Press 2.
      2. Press 1 to confirm.
    • To exit without making a change, press Stop/Exit.
  7. Press Stop/Exit.
