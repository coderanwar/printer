Send a Fax at a Specified Time (Delayed Fax)

Related Models:MFC‑L2700D / MFC‑L2701D / MFC‑L2700DW / MFC‑L2701DW / MFC‑L2740DW
You can store up to 50 faxes in the machine's memory to be sent within the next twenty-four hour period.
MFC-L2700D/MFC-L2701D/MFC-L2700DW/MFC-L2701DW
MFC-L2740DW

MFC-L2700D/MFC-L2701D/MFC-L2700DW/MFC-L2701DW

  1. Press (FAX).
  2. Load your document.
  3. Press Menu.
  4. Press or to display the Fax option, and then press OK.
  5. Press or to display the Setup Send option, and then press OK.
  6. Press or to display the Delayed Fax option, and then press OK.
  7. Enter the time you want the fax to be sent (in 24-hour format), and then press OK.
    (For example, enter 19:45 for 7:45 PM.)
  8. When finished, press 2.
  9. Enter the fax number.
  10. Press Start.
    • The number of pages you can scan into the memory depends on the amount of data printed on each page.

MFC-L2740DW

  1. Load your document.
  2. Press (Fax)>Options>Delayed Fax>Delayed Fax.
  3. Press On or Off.
  4. Press Set Time.
  5. Enter the time you want the fax to be sent (in 24-hour format), and then press OK.
    (For example, enter 19:45 for 7:45 PM.)
  6. Press OK
  7. Enter the fax number.
  8. Press Fax Start.
