WPS/AOSS™ (Wireless Network Only)

Related Models:DCP‑L2540DW / DCP‑L2541DW / HL‑L2380DW / MFC‑L2700DW / MFC‑L2701DW / MFC‑L2740DW

If your wireless LAN access point/router supports either WPS (PBC) or AOSS™ (automatic wireless mode), you can configure your Brother machine without a computer.

  • WPS refers to Wi-Fi Protected Setup™.
  • PBC refers to Push Button Configuration.
  • AOSS™ refers to AirStation One-Touch Secure System.
