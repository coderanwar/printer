Print a Report

DCP-L2520D/DCP-L2540DW/DCP-L2541DW/MFC-L2700D/MFC-L2701D/MFC-L2700DW/MFC-L2701DW
HL-L2380DW/MFC-L2740DW

DCP-L2520D/DCP-L2540DW/DCP-L2541DW/MFC-L2700D/MFC-L2701D/MFC-L2700DW/MFC-L2701DW

  1. Press Menu.
  2. Do one of the following:
    • (MFC models)

      Press or to display the Print Reports option, and then press OK.

    • (DCP models)

      Press or to display the Machine Info. option, and then press OK.

  3. Press or to display the report you want to print, and then press OK.
  4. Do one of the following:
    • If you choose XMIT Verify, do one of the following:
      • To view the Transmission Verification Report, press or to display the View on LCD, and then press OK.

      • To print the Transmission Verification Report, press or to display the Print Report, and then press OK.

        Press Start.

    • If you choose other reports, Press Start.
  5. Press Stop/Exit.

HL-L2380DW/MFC-L2740DW

  1. Press (Settings)>All Settings>Print Reports.
  2. Press or to display the report you want to print, and then press it.
  3. Do one of the following:
    • If you choose XMIT Verify, do one of the following:
      • To view the Transmission Verification Report, press View on LCD.
      • To print the Transmission Verification Report, press Print Report.
    • If you choose other reports, go to next step.
  4. Press OK.
  5. Press .
