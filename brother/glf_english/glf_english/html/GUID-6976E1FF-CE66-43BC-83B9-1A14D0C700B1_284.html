Edit Member or Group Information (Windows®)

Related Models:MFC‑L2700D / MFC‑L2701D / MFC‑L2700DW / MFC‑L2701DW / MFC‑L2740DW
  1. Do one of the following:
    • (Windows® XP, Windows Vista® and Windows® 7)

      Click (Start)>All Programs>Brother>Brother Utilities.

      Click the drop-down list and select your model name (if not already selected). Click PC-FAX Send in the left navigation bar, and then click PC-FAX Address Book.

    • (Windows® 8)

      Click (Brother Utilities), and then click the drop-down list and select your model name (if not already selected). Click PC-FAX Send in the left navigation bar, and then click PC-FAX Address Book.

    The Address Book dialog box appears.
  2. Select the member or group you want to edit.
  3. Click (Properties).
  4. Change the member or group information.
  5. Click OK.
