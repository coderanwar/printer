Change the Setting Lock Password

Related Models:MFC‑L2700D / MFC‑L2701D / MFC‑L2700DW / MFC‑L2701DW / MFC‑L2740DW
MFC-L2700D/MFC-L2701D/MFC-L2700DW/MFC-L2701DW
MFC-L2740DW

MFC-L2700D/MFC-L2701D/MFC-L2700DW/MFC-L2701DW

  1. Press Menu.
  2. Press or to display the General Setup option, and then press OK.
  3. Press or to display the Security option, and then press OK.
  4. Press or to display the Setting Lock option, and then press OK.
  5. Press or to select the Set Password option, and then press OK.
  6. Enter the current four-digit password.
  7. Press OK.
  8. Enter a new four-digit password.
  9. Press OK.
  10. When the LCD displays Verify:, re-enter the password.
  11. Press OK.
  12. Press Stop/Exit.

MFC-L2740DW

  1. Press (Settings)>All Settings>General Setup>Setting Lock>Set Password.
  2. Enter the current four-digit password.
  3. Press OK.
  4. Enter a new four-digit password.
  5. Press OK.
  6. When the LCD displays Verify:, re-enter the password.
  7. Press OK.
  8. Press .
