Copy on Both Sides of the Paper (2-sided Copy)

Reduce the amount of paper you use by copying onto both sides of the paper.
  • You must choose a 2-sided copy layout from the following options before you can start 2-sided copying.
  • The layout of your original document determines which 2-sided copy layout you should choose.
  • To use the automatic 2-sided copy feature, you must load your document in the ADF.
  • To use the manual 2-sided copy feature, place your document on the scanner glass.

DCP-L2520D/DCP-L2540DW/DCP-L2541DW/MFC-L2700D/MFC-L2701D/MFC-L2700DW/MFC-L2701DW

  1. Load your document.
  2. Do one of the following:

    • For MFC models

      Press (COPY).

      Use the dial pad to enter the number of copies you want.

    • For DCP models

      Press or to enter the number of copies you want.

  3. Do one of the following:

    • Press 2-sided.

    • Press Options.

      Press or to select 2-sided, and then press OK.

  4. Press or to select 1sided→2sided L, 1sided→2sided S, or Off, and then press OK.
  5. Press Start.
    If you placed the document in the ADF, the machine scans the pages and starts printing.
    If you are using the scanner glass, go to the next step.
  6. Repeat the following steps for each page of the layout:
    • For MFC models
      1. After the machine scans the page, press 1 to scan the next page.
      2. Place the next page on the scanner glass, and then press OK to scan the page.
    • For DCP models
      1. After the machine scans the page, press to select the Yes option.
      2. Place the next page on the scanner glass, and then press OK to scan the page.
  7. After scanning all the pages, do one of the following:
    • For MFC models

      Press 2 to select the No option.

    • For DCP models

      Press to select the No option.

HL-L2380DW/MFC-L2740DW

  1. Load your document.
  2. Press (Copy).
  3. Enter the number of copies.
  4. Press Options.
  5. Press or to display the 2-sided Copy option, and then press 2-sided Copy.
  6. Press or to display the layout options, and then press the option you want.
  7. When finished, press OK.
  8. Press Start. If you placed the document in the ADF, the machine scans the pages and starts printing.
    If you are using the scanner glass, go to the next step.
  9. Repeat the following steps for each page of the document:
    1. After the machine scans the page, press Scan to scan the next page.
    2. Place the next page on the scanner glass, and then press OK to scan the page.
  10. After scanning all the pages, press Complete to finish.
