Test Print

If there are problems with print quality, follow these instructions to do a test print.
DCP-L2520D/DCP-L2540DW/DCP-L2541DW/MFC-L2700D/MFC-L2701D/MFC-L2700DW/MFC-L2701DW
HL-L2380DW/MFC-L2740DW

DCP-L2520D/DCP-L2540DW/DCP-L2541DW/MFC-L2700D/MFC-L2701D/MFC-L2700DW/MFC-L2701DW

  1. Press Menu.
  2. Press or to display the Printer option, and then press OK.
  3. (For Printer Emulation Supported models)
    Press or to display the Print Options option, and then press OK.
  4. Press or to display the Test Print option, and then press OK.
  5. Press Start.
    The machine prints a Test Print page.
  6. Press Stop/Exit.

HL-L2380DW/MFC-L2740DW

  1. Press (Settings)>All Settings>Printer>Print Options>Test Print.
  2. Press OK.
  3. Press .
