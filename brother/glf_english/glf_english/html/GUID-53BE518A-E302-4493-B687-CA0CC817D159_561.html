Recall Shortcuts

Related Models:HL‑L2380DW / MFC‑L2740DW
The Shortcut settings are displayed on the Shortcuts screen. To recall a Shortcut, simply press the Shortcut name.
  1. Press (Shortcuts).
  2. Press or to display the Shortcut you want to recall.
  3. Press the Shortcut.
