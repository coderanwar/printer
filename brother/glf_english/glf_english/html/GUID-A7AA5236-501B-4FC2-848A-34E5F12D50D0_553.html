Add Copy Shortcuts

Related Models:HL‑L2380DW / MFC‑L2740DW
  1. Press (Shortcuts).
  2. Press where you have not added a Shortcut.
  3. Press or to display Copy, and then press Copy.
  4. Read the information on the LCD, and then confirm it by pressing OK.
  5. Press the setting you want to change.
  6. Press or to display the available options for the setting, and then press the option you want to set.
    Repeat these steps until you have selected all the settings for this Shortcut.
  7. When you have finished changing settings, press Save as Shortcut.
  8. Read and confirm the displayed list of settings you have selected, and then press OK.
  9. Enter a name for the Shortcut using the LCD, and then press OK.
  10. Press OK to save your Shortcut.
