Set the Check Size

Your Brother machine's Check Size setting causes the machine to display a message when you remove the paper tray from the machine, asking if you changed the paper size.

The default setting is On.

DCP-L2520D/DCP-L2540DW/DCP-L2541DW/MFC-L2700D/MFC-L2701D/MFC-L2700DW/MFC-L2701DW

  1. Press Menu.
  2. Press or to display the General Setup option, and then press OK.
  3. Press or to display the Paper option, and then press OK.
  4. Press or to display the Check Size option, and then press OK.
  5. Press or to select the On or Off option, and then press OK.
  6. Press Stop/Exit.
