Redial a Fax Number

Related Models:MFC‑L2700D / MFC‑L2701D / MFC‑L2700DW / MFC‑L2701DW / MFC‑L2740DW
If you are sending a fax automatically and the line is busy, the machine will redial up to three times at five minute intervals.

  • Redial only works if you dialled from the machine.

  • In real time transmission, the automatic redial feature does not work when you are using the scanner glass.

MFC-L2700D/MFC-L2701D/MFC-L2700DW/MFC-L2701DW
MFC-L2740DW

MFC-L2700D/MFC-L2701D/MFC-L2700DW/MFC-L2701DW

  1. Load your document.
  2. Press (FAX).
  3. Press Redial/Pause.
  4. Press or to select the number you want to dial, and then press OK.
  5. Press Start.

MFC-L2740DW

  1. Load your document.
  2. Press (Fax).
  3. Press Redial.
    The LCD shows the last number called.
  4. Press Fax Start.
