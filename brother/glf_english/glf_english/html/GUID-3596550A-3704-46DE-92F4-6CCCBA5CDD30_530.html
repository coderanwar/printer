Adjust the Beeper Volume

Related Models:HL‑L2380DW / MFC‑L2700D / MFC‑L2701D / MFC‑L2700DW / MFC‑L2701DW / MFC‑L2740DW
When the beeper is on, the machine will beep when you press a button, make a mistake, or after you send or receive a fax. You can choose from a range of volume levels.
MFC-L2700D/MFC-L2701D/MFC-L2700DW/MFC-L2701DW

MFC-L2700D/MFC-L2701D/MFC-L2700DW/MFC-L2701DW

  1. Press Menu.
  2. Press or to display the General Setup option, and then press OK.
  3. Press or to display the Volume option, and then press OK.
  4. Press or to display the Beeper option, and then press OK.
  5. Press or to display the High, Med, Low, or Off option, and then press OK.
  6. Press Stop/Exit.
