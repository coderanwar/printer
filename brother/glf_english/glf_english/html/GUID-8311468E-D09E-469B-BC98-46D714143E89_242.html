Dial a Number Using One Touch Dial

Related Models:MFC‑L2700D / MFC‑L2701D / MFC‑L2700DW / MFC‑L2701DW
  1. Press (FAX).
  2. Load your document.
  3. Press the One Touch button assigned to the fax or telephone number you want to dial.
    To dial One Touch numbers 1 to 4, press the One Touch button. To dial One Touch numbers 5 to 8, hold down Shift as you press the One Touch button.
  4. Press Start.
