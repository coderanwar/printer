Supplies

When the time comes to replace supplies such as the toner or drum, an error message will appear on your machine's control panel or in the Status Monitor. For more information about the supplies for your machine, visit http://www.brother.com/original/index.html or contact your local Brother reseller.

The toner cartridge and drum unit are two separate parts.
