Store Numbers

Related Models:MFC‑L2700D / MFC‑L2701D / MFC‑L2700DW / MFC‑L2701DW / MFC‑L2703DW / MFC‑L2720DW / MFC‑L2740DW

Store fax and telephone numbers in the machine for quick dialling and broadcasting faxes to groups. When you dial an stored number, the LCD displays the number.

If you lose electrical power, the Address Book numbers that are in the machine's memory will not be lost.
