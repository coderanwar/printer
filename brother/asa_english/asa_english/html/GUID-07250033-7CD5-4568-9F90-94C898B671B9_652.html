APIPA

If you do not assign an IP address manually (using the control panel [for LCD models] of the machine or the BRAdmin software) or automatically (using a DHCP, BOOTP, or RARP server), the Automatic Private IP Addressing (APIPA) protocol will automatically assign an IP address from the range 169.254.1.0 to 169.254.254.255.
