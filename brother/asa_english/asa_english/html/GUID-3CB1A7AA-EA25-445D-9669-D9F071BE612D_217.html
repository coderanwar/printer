Set the 2-sided Printing for Received Faxes

Related Models:MFC‑L2700D / MFC‑L2701D / MFC‑L2700DW / MFC‑L2701DW / MFC‑L2703DW / MFC‑L2720DW / MFC‑L2740DW
Your machine prints received faxes on both sides of the paper when 2-sided is set to On.

  • Use A4 sized paper (60 to 105 g/m2) for this function.

    (For Philippines; Letter, Legal, or Folio)

  • When 2-sided printing is enabled, incoming faxes are automatically reduced to fit the paper in the paper tray.
MFC-L2700D/MFC-L2701D/MFC-L2700DW/MFC-L2701DW/MFC-L2703DW

MFC-L2700D/MFC-L2701D/MFC-L2700DW/MFC-L2701DW/MFC-L2703DW

  1. Press (FAX).
  2. Press Menu.
  3. Press or to display the Fax option, and then press OK.
  4. Press or to display the Setup Receive option, and then press OK.
  5. Press or to display the 2-sided option, and then press OK.
  6. Press or to display the On (or Off) option, and then press OK.
  7. Press Stop/Exit.

MFC-L2720DW/MFC-L2740DW

  1. Press (Settings)>All Settings>Fax>Setup Receive>2-sided.
  2. Press On or Off.
  3. Press .
