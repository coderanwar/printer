Change the Paper Size

When you change the size of the paper you load in the tray, you must change the paper size setting on the LCD at the same time.
  • When an appropriate size is not in the tray, received faxes will be stored in the machine’s memory and Size mismatch will appear on the LCD.
  • If the tray is out of paper and received faxes are in the machine’s memory, No Paper will appear on the LCD. Insert paper in the empty tray.
