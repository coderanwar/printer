Send a Fax Overseas

Related Models:MFC‑L2700D / MFC‑L2701D / MFC‑L2700DW / MFC‑L2701DW / MFC‑L2703DW / MFC‑L2720DW / MFC‑L2740DW
If you are having difficulty sending a fax overseas due to a bad connection, then turning on Overseas Mode may help.
MFC-L2700D/MFC-L2701D/MFC-L2700DW/MFC-L2701DW/MFC-L2703DW
MFC-L2720DW/MFC-L2740DW

MFC-L2700D/MFC-L2701D/MFC-L2700DW/MFC-L2701DW/MFC-L2703DW

  1. Press (FAX).
  2. Load your document.
  3. Press Menu.
  4. Press or to display the Fax option, and then press OK.
  5. Press or to display the Setup Send option, and then press OK.
  6. Press or to display the Overseas Mode option, and then press OK.
  7. Press or to display the Next Fax:On, On, or Off option, and then press OK.
    Option
    Description
    Next Fax:On

    The Overseas Mode will only be active for your next fax.

    On

    The Overseas Mode will be active.

    Off

    The Overseas Mode will be inactive.

  8. Press OK.
  9. When finished, press 2.
  10. Enter the fax number.
  11. Press Start.

MFC-L2720DW/MFC-L2740DW

  1. Load your document.
  2. Press (Fax)>Options>Overseas Mode.
  3. Press On or Off.
  4. Press OK
  5. Enter the fax number.
  6. Press Fax Start.
