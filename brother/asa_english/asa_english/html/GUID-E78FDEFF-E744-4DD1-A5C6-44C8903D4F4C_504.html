Remove Small Scraps of Paper Jammed in the ADF

Related Models:DCP‑L2540DW / DCP‑L2541DW / MFC‑L2700DW / MFC‑L2701DW / MFC‑L2700D / MFC‑L2701D / MFC‑L2703DW / MFC‑L2720DW / MFC‑L2740DW
  1. Lift the document cover.
  2. Insert a piece of stiff paper, such as cardstock, into the ADF to push any small paper scraps through.
  3. Close the document cover.
  4. Do one of the following:

    • For 2-Line LCD models

      Press Stop/Exit.
