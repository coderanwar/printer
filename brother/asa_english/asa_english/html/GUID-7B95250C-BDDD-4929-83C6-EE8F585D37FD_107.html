Print the Internal Font List

Related Models:DCP‑L2540DW / DCP‑L2541DW / HL‑L2380DW / MFC‑L2720DW / MFC‑L2740DW
Print a list of the machine's internal (also called resident) fonts to see how each font looks before you select it.
DCP-L2540DW/DCP-L2541DW
HL-L2380DW/MFC-L2720DW/MFC-L2740DW

DCP-L2540DW/DCP-L2541DW

  1. Press Menu.
  2. Press or to display the Printer option, and then press OK.
  3. Press or to display the Print Options option, and then press OK.
  4. Press or to display the INTERNAL FONT option, and then press OK.
  5. Press the HP LaserJet or BR-Script 3 option.
  6. Press Stop/Exit.

HL-L2380DW/MFC-L2720DW/MFC-L2740DW

  1. Press (Settings)>All Settings>Printer>Print Options>Internal Font.
  2. Press the HP LaserJet or BR-Script 3 option.
  3. Press OK.
  4. Press .
