Clean the Scanner

  1. Press and hold to turn off the machine.
  2. (For automatic 2-sided scanning models) Unfold the ADF document support (1), and then open the ADF cover (2). Clean the white bar (3) and the scanner glass strip (4) underneath it with a soft lint-free cloth moistened with water. Close the ADF cover and fold the ADF document support.
  3. Lift the document cover (1). Clean the white plastic surface (2) and scanner glass (3) underneath it with a soft lint-free cloth moistened with water.
  4. In the ADF unit, clean the white bar (1) and the scanner glass strip (2) underneath it with a soft lint-free cloth moistened with water.
  5. Close the document cover.
  6. Press to turn on the machine.
