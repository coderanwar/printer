DCP-L2520D/DCP-L2540DW/DCP-L2541DW/MFC-L2700D/MFC-L2701D/MFC-L2700DW/MFC-L2701DW/MFC-L2703DW

  1. Press Menu.
  2. Press or to display the Machine Info. option, and then press OK.
  3. Press or to display the Parts Life option, and then press OK.
  4. Press or to select the Toner or Drum option, and then press OK.
    The LCD displays the approximate remaining toner or drum life.
  5. Press Stop/Exit.

HL-L2380DW/MFC-L2720DW/MFC-L2740DW

  1. Press (Settings)>All Settings>Machine Info.>Parts Life.
  2. The LCD displays the approximate remaining drum life.
  3. Press .
    To display the approximate remaining life of toner cartridge, press on the LCD.
