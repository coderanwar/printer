Turn Setting Lock Off

Related Models:MFC‑L2700D / MFC‑L2701D / MFC‑L2700DW / MFC‑L2701DW / MFC‑L2703DW / MFC‑L2720DW / MFC‑L2740DW
MFC-L2700D/MFC-L2701D/MFC-L2700DW/MFC-L2701DW/MFC-L2703DW
MFC-L2720DW/MFC-L2740DW

MFC-L2700D/MFC-L2701D/MFC-L2700DW/MFC-L2701DW/MFC-L2703DW

  1. Press Menu.
  2. Press or to display the General Setup option, and then press OK.
  3. Press or to display the Security option, and then press OK.
  4. Press or to display the Setting Lock option, and then press OK.
  5. Enter the current four-digit password.
  6. Press OK.
  7. Press OK when the LCD shows Off.
  8. Press Stop/Exit.

MFC-L2720DW/MFC-L2740DW

  1. Press on the LCD.
  2. Enter the current four-digit password.
  3. Press OK.
