Initial Setup for Email or I-Fax

Related Models:MFC‑L2720DW / MFC‑L2740DW

Before sending your I-Fax, use the control panel, Web Based Management, or Remote Setup to configure the following optional items:

  • E-mail Subject
  • Limit E-mail Size
  • Request Delivery Notification (Send)

If you are unsure of any of these items, contact your system administrator.
