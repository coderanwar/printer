Add Scan Shortcuts

Related Models:HL‑L2380DW / MFC‑L2720DW / MFC‑L2740DW

If you use Scan to E-mail Server function, the Internet Fax (I-Fax) option must be downloaded.

  1. Press (Shortcuts).
  2. Press where you have not added a Shortcut.
  3. Press or to display Scan, and then press Scan.
  4. Press or to display to File, to OCR, to Image, to E-mail, to E-mail Server (available after Internet Fax (I-Fax) is downloaded), or to FTP, and then press the option you want.
  5. Do one of the following:
    • If you pressed to File, to OCR, to Image or to E-mail, read the information on the LCD.
      • When the machine is connected to a PC using a USB connection, press OK to confirm <USB> is chosen as the PC Name.
      • When the machine is connected to a network, do the following steps:

        1. Press or to display the PC Name, and then press it.

        2. Press OK to confirm the PC Name you have chosen.

    • If you pressed to E-mail Server (available after Internet Fax (I-Fax) is downloaded), read the information on the LCD, and then do the following steps:

      1. Press OK to confirm it.

        • To enter an E-mail address manually, press Manual. Enter the E-mail address using the LCD, and then press OK.

        • To enter an E-mail address from the Address Book, do the following steps:

          1. Press Address Book.

          2. Press or to display the E-mail address you want, and then press it.

          3. Press OK.

      2. After you confirm the E-mail address you entered, press OK.

        Press to view the list of addresses you have entered.

      3. To change the settings, press Options, and then select the option you want to change.

      4. Follow the machine's instructions.

      5. Review your settings on the LCD, and then press OK.

    • If you pressed to FTP, read the information on the LCD, and then do the following steps:

      1. Press OK to confirm it.

      2. Press or to display the Profile Name, and then press it.

      3. Press OK to confirm the Profile Name you have selected.

        To add a Shortcut for to FTP, you must have added the Profile Name beforehand.

  6. Enter a name for the shortcut using the LCD, and then press OK.
  7. Press OK to save your Shortcut.
