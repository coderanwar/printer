Adjust the Copy Contrast

If your document is very light or very dark, changing the contrast may improve the quality of the copy.
DCP-L2520D/DCP-L2540DW/DCP-L2541DW/MFC-L2700D/MFC-L2701D/MFC-L2700DW/MFC-L2701DW/MFC-L2703DW
HL-L2380DW/MFC-L2720DW/MFC-L2740DW

DCP-L2520D/DCP-L2540DW/DCP-L2541DW/MFC-L2700D/MFC-L2701D/MFC-L2700DW/MFC-L2701DW/MFC-L2703DW

  1. Load your document.
  2. Do one of the following:

    • For MFC models

      Press (COPY).

      Use the dial pad to enter the number of copies you want.

    • For DCP models

      Press or to enter the number of copies you want.

  3. Press Options.
  4. Press or to select Contrast, and then press OK.
  5. Press or ( or for some models) to select a level of contrast, and then press OK.
  6. Press Start.

HL-L2380DW/MFC-L2720DW/MFC-L2740DW

  1. Load your document.
  2. Press (Copy).
  3. Enter the number of copies.
  4. Press Options.
  5. Press or to display the Contrast option, and then press Contrast.
  6. Press a level of contrast.
  7. When finished, press OK.
  8. Press Start.
