Transfer the Fax Journal Report to Another Fax Machine

Related Models:MFC‑L2700D / MFC‑L2701D / MFC‑L2700DW / MFC‑L2701DW / MFC‑L2703DW / MFC‑L2720DW / MFC‑L2740DW
If you have not set up your Station ID, you cannot enter fax transfer mode.
MFC-L2700D/MFC-L2701D/MFC-L2700DW/MFC-L2701DW/MFC-L2703DW
MFC-L2720DW/MFC-L2740DW

MFC-L2700D/MFC-L2701D/MFC-L2700DW/MFC-L2701DW/MFC-L2703DW

  1. Press Menu.
  2. Press or to display the Service option, and then press OK.
  3. Press OK to select Data Transfer.
  4. Press or to display the Report Trans. option, and then press OK.
  5. Enter the destination fax number for the Fax Journal.
  6. Press Start.

MFC-L2720DW/MFC-L2740DW

  1. Press (Settings)>All Settings>Service>Data Transfer>Report Transfer.
  2. Enter the destination fax number for the Fax Journal.
  3. Press Fax Start.
