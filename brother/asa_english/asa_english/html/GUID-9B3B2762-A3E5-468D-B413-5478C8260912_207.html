Save Fax Options as a Shortcut

Related Models:MFC‑L2720DW / MFC‑L2740DW
Store the fax options you use frequently by saving them as a Shortcut.
  1. Press (Fax).
  2. Enter the fax number.
  3. Press Options.
  4. Press or to display the setting you want to change, and then press it. Then press the new option.
    Repeat this step for each setting you want to change.
  5. When finished, press OK.
  6. Press Save as Shortcut.
  7. Confirm the displayed list of options you selected for the Shortcut, and then press OK.
  8. Enter a name for the Shortcut using the LCD, and then press OK.
  9. Press OK to confirm.
