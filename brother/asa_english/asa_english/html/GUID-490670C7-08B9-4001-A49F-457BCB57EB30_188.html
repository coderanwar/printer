Send a 2-sided Document from the ADF

Related Models:MFCâ€‘L2740DW
You must select the 2-sided scanning format before you send a 2-sided document. The format you selected will depend on the layout of your 2-sided document.
  1. Load your document.
  2. Press (Fax)>Options>2-sided Fax.
  3. Do one of the following:
    • If your document is flipped on the long edge, press the 2-sided Scan: Long Edge option.
    • If your document is flipped on the short edge, press the 2-sided Scan: Short Edge option.
  4. Press OK
  5. Enter the fax number.
  6. Press Fax Start.
