Copy Document Using ControlCenter4 Advanced Mode (Windows速)

You can configure the advanced copy settings from your computer.

Select Advanced Mode as the mode setting for ControlCenter4.

  1. Load your document.
  2. Click the (ControlCenter4) icon in the task tray, and then click Open.
  3. Click the PC-Copy tab.
  4. To change the copy settings, right-click the button you want to change, and then change the settings.
    To preview and configure the scanned image, select the PreScan check box. Click OK.
  5. Click the Monochrome Copy, 2 in 1 Copy, or 4 in 1 Copy option.
    If you have selected the PreScan, the PreScan dialog box appears. Click the PreScan button and then click Scan.
    The scanned image is printed.
