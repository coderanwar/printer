Change 2-Sided Print Settings

Change the settings for printing on both sides of the paper from your machine's control panel.

Settings in the printer driver always take priority over settings you change manually on the control panel. If you do not print 2-sided frequently, select None for the 2-sided / Booklet pull down list in the printer driver, and use the control panel to turn it on manually only when you want to print on both sides of the paper.

  2. Press or to display the Printer option, and then press OK.

  4. Press or to display the Off, On(Long Edge), or On(Short Edge) option, and then press OK.
  5. Press Stop/Exit.
