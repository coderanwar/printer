Dial a Number Using Speed Dial

Related Models:MFC‑L2700D / MFC‑L2701D / MFC‑L2700DW / MFC‑L2701DW / MFC‑L2703DW
  1. Press (FAX).
  2. Load your document.
  3. Do one of the following:

    • Press twice and enter the three-digit Speed Dial code.

    • Press .

      Press the dial pad buttons for the first few letters of the name, and then press OK.

      Press or to scroll until you find the name you are looking for, and then press OK.

  4. Press Start.
