Set Fax to Server to On

Related Models:MFC‑L2720DW / MFC‑L2740DW
Store the prefix/suffix address for the fax server in your Brother machine.
  1. Press (Settings)>All Settings>Network>Fax to Server.
  2. Press On.
  3. Press Prefix.
  4. Enter the prefix (up to 40 characters) using the keyboard on the LCD, and then press OK.
  5. Press Suffix.
  6. Enter the suffix (up to 40 characters) using the keyboard on the LCD, and then press OK.
  7. Press .
