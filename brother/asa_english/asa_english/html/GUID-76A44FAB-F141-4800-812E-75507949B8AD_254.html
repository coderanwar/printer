Store Address Book Numbers from Caller ID History

Related Models:MFC‑L2720DW / MFC‑L2740DW
If you have Caller ID subscriber service from your telephone company, you can store Address Book numbers from incoming calls in the Caller ID history.
  1. Press (Fax)>Call History>Caller ID history.
  2. Press or to display the name or number you want to store, and then press it.
  3. Press Edit>Add to Address Book>Name.
  4. Enter the name (up to 15 characters) using the LCD. Press OK.
  5. Press OK to confirm the fax or telephone number you want to store.
  6. Press .
